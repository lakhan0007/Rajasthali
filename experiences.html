Experience the culture and amazement of Rajasthali!

Unique Dining Experience

Indulge in a once in a lifetime opportunity as we curate an extravagant, ubiquitous and certainly personalized dining experience at Rajasthali. Be it against the picturesque backdrop of the Aravallis or the breathtaking silhouette of the hotel in the evening, we strive, with our entire team to bring to you the Unique Dining Experience unlike anything else.

Yoga

Irrespective of whether you are a beginner or have practiced Yoga for many years, our Yoga sessions are customized as per your requirement and give you a refreshed mind, body and soul.

Sports

Our lush green lawns ensure there is never a lazy moment for those who love to keep their adrenaline pump and want to get involved in leisurely as well as competitive sports. We provide our guests with Cricket Kits, Badminton, a volley ball court, football, and much more to make sure our guests make the most of their time at our resort.

Biking Trail

In mood for some adventure while you are at the Aravalis? For the thrill seekers, our trail bikes give you a perfect opportunity to explore the Aravalis and tread on to a bike ride amidst the beautiful Aravalli.

Kalbelia

Visiting the dessert land and yet not seen the beauty of the dessert? At Rajasthali, we believe in curating the local experience for our guests to give them a glimpse into the many colors of Rajasthan! Visit the local village for a surreal experience or be a part of the Kalbelia dancers as they groove to the music of the dessert land in the evening!

Jeep Safari to Amer Fort

Let our team curate an unforgettable memory for you of going on a jeep safari to the Amer Fort – the most regal and heralded fort of Jaipur. Watch the sunrise from the jeep at Amer while you sip on a cup of hot tea, brough along by the server.
