Weddings at Rajasthali!

Jaipur is one of the most coveted destinations for regal and princely wedding celebrations. And what better an opportunity to celebrate the most important day of your life than in the lap of the scenic Aravalli nature while indulging in a luxurious experience.
With a plethora of spaces starting from a spacious banquet hall to a ubiquitous lawn, with its magnificent arches and a dreamlike royal structure, Rajasthali is the destinations for the dream weddings. (Send us a query and we will plan for you)

Open Lawn

The 2 spacious lawn out in the open, in front of the resort and behind it are one of a kind with its regal pool setting and a picturesque view of the Aravalli behind, making it one of the most quintessential venue for traditional wedding celebrations. With each lawn being no less than 20,000 sq ft, it provides the perfect venue for the grand wedding celebrations that define the Indian wedding.

Deewan-E-Khas

We welcome you to our newly launched banquet with a perfect confluence of modern ambience with a flavor of luxury and royalty. Inaugurated by Manushi Chhillar, Miss India World 2017, Deewan – E- Khas is the perfect venue for holding your special occasions.

Meeting and Conference Hall

Offering the perfect combination of efficiency and style, our meeting and conference rooms boast of the quintessential business settings that are matched by spotless services. From start to finish, our team stands by you to ensure your meeting performs flawlessly.
